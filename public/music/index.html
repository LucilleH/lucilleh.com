music

I start writing music since high school, but only recently did I start recording them. My music style has been changing over the years -- from rock to alternative blues to country. But the following pieces are the once that suits my voice (I can't sing rock sorry ). These recordings are not professional enough, since I'm just using my laptop, my guitar, and myself, nothing else. There's no auto-tuning, no voice polishing, but I would say they sound very original and natural. Maybe someday I will find/build a recording studio and do a professional round, but for now, this is enough, for the purpose of sharing with you.

Enjoy my music, and if you like them, please let me know and I'll feel better about myself haha. Or you can follow my music on my soundcloud.
